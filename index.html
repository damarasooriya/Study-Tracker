User Guide
🚀
Getting Started
Welcome to Study Tracker! This app helps you organize your study sessions, track confidence levels, and prepare for exams. Start by adding a subject on the main screen.
📚
Subjects & Lessons
Once you create a subject, open it to add your lessons. You can split lessons into sections, add notes, and save resource links. Everything is kept neatly in one place.
🔄
Tracking Confidence
Tap the circle icon next to any lesson to mark your confidence level. It cycles through: Not Studied → Needs Work → Working → Confident.
☁️
Subject Library
Click the cloud icon in the top right to download fully structured subjects made by other students, or share your own!
💾
Data & Privacy
Your data never leaves your device. If you switch phones, open Settings (⚙️) to Export your data, then Import it on your new device.
